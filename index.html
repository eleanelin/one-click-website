Keeping Vintage in Mind

Improving accessibility and breaking barriers.

One Click serves as a photographer companion to simplify the tedious, monotonous process of photographing clothes from various angles, editing those images, and generating information to list them on a storefront. Our product allows sellers to focus on the creative process of sourcing, rather than taking the perfect picture.

It's essentially a portable automated photo studio. It's compact but advanced, with features such as telescoping poles for quick setup anywhere, an automatic rotating clothing pole for getting photos at all angles, and integration with software for a streamlined marketing process. The software component provides an analysis of the photos, generating details and price estimations.

Automatic, High-Quality Photography

Easily adjustable photo settings and smart-focus.

When you first enter the photo studio, you'll be greeted by a user-friendly interface that allows you to customize your photo settings with just a few clicks. Effects range from lighting to shutter speed, ensuring you get perfect shots from any angle.

Secure Storage on the Cloud

Access and store your photos easily after taking them.

One Click provides secure cloud storage for all your photos, ensuring they are safe and accessible from anywhere as long as they're connected to your account. You're able to go back at any time to view, edit, or export your photos.

Seamless Device Integration

Connect One Click to your phone for remote control.

One Click can be easily integrated with your device with the bluetooth function, allowing you to control the photo studio remotely. The settings can be adjusted to control the rotation of the clothing pole, desired clothing details, and price ranges.
