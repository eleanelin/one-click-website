Keeping Vintage in Mind

Improving accessibility and breaking barriers.

One Click serves as a photographer companion to simplify the tedious, monotonous process of photographing clothes from various angles, editing those images, and listing them to a storefront. Our product allows sellers to focus on the creative process of sourcing, rather than taking the perfect picture.

One Click is essentially a portable automated photo studio. Compact but advanced, with features such as easily telescoping poles for quick setup anywhere, an automatic rotating pole for getting photos at all angles, and integration with software for a streamlined marketing process.
